Tiffin Art Guild & Gallery


The Tiffin Art Guild has on-going art exhibits of member and guest artwork such as oil, acrylic & watercolor paintings, photography, jewelry, glass and wood sculpture and more. Most of which is for sale. In addition, they have special exhibitions and artist shows on display throughout the year. TAG has a wide selection of hand-crafted works are available in the gift shop and offers classes for all ages and skill level.
