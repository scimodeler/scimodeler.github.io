Gem Yoga and Life Enrichment


Established in 2014, Gem Yoga and Life Enrichment became Tiffin's first studio dedicated to the practice of yoga. Since then, we have grown into a center which focuses on cultivating well-being through a variety of services including: Yoga Classes; Private Therapeutic Yoga for Individuals; Meditation; Aromatherapy; Reiki; Massage Therapy; Reflexology; CranioSacral Therapy; Specialty Workshops; Day Retreats; Community Outreach and more. We tailor our classes and private services to the unique needs of our clients. Please visit our website for the most current listing of services and events being offered.
